About

PipeCount AI

v1.0 · build 2026.06 · prototype

PipeCount AI counts visible pipe openings from a single photo — built for warehouse stock audits on loose, unbundled pipe stacks. It is not intended for factory production counting or loading reconciliation, and bundled CPVC/UPVC stock and container-loaded pipes fall outside its scope.

How it works
  • Capture the exposed ends of a pipe stack with the camera or pick a photo
  • On-device detection finds each circular opening and assigns it an ID
  • Telescoped (pipe-inside-pipe) openings are flagged and excluded by default
  • Tap + Add to mark a missed pipe, or Delete to remove a false detection
  • Save the audit — it's logged to History with the date, time and count
Good to know
  • Accuracy depends on lighting, angle and how exposed the pipe ends are
  • Always do a quick visual spot-check before relying on the count
  • This is a prototype build — treat counts as an audit aid, not a final record
